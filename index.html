WHOLESOME MARKET

Sunshine Wholesome Market is a community shop & juice bar that offers a variety of whole food choices. Specializing in organic & gluten free products, we also boast a gourmet fresh sandwich bar featuring organic ingredients and condiments alongside local Ontario meats raised ethically and without hormones/steroids.

Our goal is to share amazing food and support products that are fair trade, sustainable, good for our planet and excellent for our bodies. We aim to educate people on wise and healthy food choices.
